How to Use Engineerus Quest

Getting Started

  1. Complete Your Profile - Your progress is automatically tracked
  2. Complete Quests - Earn XP and level up by finishing engineering tasks
  3. Use AI Assistant - Ask questions about any engineering topic
  4. Study Books - Access curated books for Mechanical, Electrical, and Robotics
  5. Maintain Streak - Login daily to keep your streak alive!
